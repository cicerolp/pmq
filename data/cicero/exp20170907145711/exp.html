1 Description   export

Test the queries on uniform data. And compare the folling performances.

Use 10**8 elements.

  • PMQ / GEOHASH
  • BTREE
  • RTREE - quadratic algorithm
  • RTREE - quadratic algorithm with bulk loading

Use the refinement level = 8
